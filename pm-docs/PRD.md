# Project Title: Easy Dorm Recipes Digital Cookbook

## The goal of this website is to provide easy dorm food recipes with ingredients, instructions, cooking times, and anything else necessary to providing a cooking experience that can be easy to follow.

## Core Features
- List of 6 recipes (breakfast, lunch, dinner) - Include instructions, ingredients, cooking times measurements (These do not need to be real recipes - can be pulled off the internet or made up)
- Navigation bar to direct to all
- Images of food and ingredients with hover cards that show the name when hovered over
- An interactive form that can leave a rating and any questions, etc. 
- Display changes for accessibility: ex. Dark mode toggle
- Print recipe button

## Pages
- Home/About/Contact (all in one)
- Breakfast Recipes
- Lunch Recipes
- Dinner Recipes
